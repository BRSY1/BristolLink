How does it work?

  1. Sign up on Bristol Link, put down the bristol uni email of your crush on link.
  2. Your crush gets an email from us saying, someone has a crush on them (we don't say who). They'll be prompted put down the email of their crush.
  3. If they put down your name, you'll both get an email saying it's a match. Otherwise, we'll let you know they're not interested in you and you get another chance. They will not know you sent it, unless you match.

How do I get my crush's bristol uni email?

Search their full name on Outlook and you'll find their email
